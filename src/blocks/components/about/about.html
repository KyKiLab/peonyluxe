About our company

The work of our photographers can be described as a combination of artistic photography and photojournalism. The goal is to create beautifully designed images while maintaining the authenticity of the moments. With over 10 years of professional experience, we provide a wide range of photography services such as wedding photojournalism, wedding or engagement preparation, honeymoon and travel photography.
