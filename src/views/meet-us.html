Features of our work

Peony Luxe has over 10 years of experience in wedding photography and videos. Our employees know how the whole process works and how to catch a shot at any given moment. Experienced specialists skillfully handle their equipment, understand the meaning of technical parameters and functions of the devices. Wedding photographers know how to work with both natural light and artificial lighting. They instantly react to what is happening at the moment. They know how to capture interesting and funny moments, maneuvering between guests and the main heroes of the occasion. Professionals know how to work with graphic editors and other programs. They keep abreast of trends in the photography world and know how to choose the right angle for a photo frame.

Qualities of real professionals

The team of the best photographers and videographers have endurance, calmness, stress resistance, excellent organizational skills and an ability to pull themselves together at the right time. Peony Luxe will ensure your event is run smoothly. If a camera fails, there is always an additional one in the photographer’s bag.
